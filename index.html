Welcome Home

Where Faith Meets Purpose

Welcome to Anointed Rivers Ministry, where faith meets purpose. Here, we gather to uplift, inspire, and walk together in God's light, building a community rooted in love, compassion, and spiritual growth.

Through dynamic worship, teachings, and fellowship, we strive to nurture every member's spiritual journey. From youth programs to Sunday school, from praise and worship performances to media outreach, we provide a space for everyone to grow, connect and serve.

Join us as we walk this path of faith together. Whether it's through attending services, participating in ministries, or simply sharing in our message, you're always welcome to become part of a community where God's love transforms lives everyday.
