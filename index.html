Welcome to Anointed Rivers Ministry, where faith meets purpose. Here, we gather to uplift, inspire, and walk together in Gods light, building a community rooted in love, compssion, and spiritual growth.

Through dynamic worship, teachings, and fellowship, we strive to nuture every member's spiritual journey. From youth programs to Sunday school, from praise and worship perfomances to media outreach, we provide a space for everyone to grow, connect and serve.

Join us as we walk this path of faith together. Whether it's through attending services, participating in ministries, or simply sharing in our message, you're always welcome to become part of a community where God's love transforms lives everyday. Here friendships are forged, faith is strengthened, hearts encouraged, and hope is renewed. Welcome home to Annointed Rivers Ministry.

About Us

Anointed Rivers is a Christ-centered ministry shaped by humility, refined by excellence, and driven by obedience to God’s call. Our doors are open to all who seek a deeper connection with God and a vibrant, loving community. Here, every story matters and every journey is celebrated as part of a greater purpose.

We exist to reach the lost, disciple believers, and raise leaders who are deeply rooted in God’s Word and prepared to influence their communities and the nations. Through passionate worship and heartfelt service, we ignite a spark of hope that radiates beyond our walls. Together, we are building a legacy of faith that transforms lives for generations to come.

Here, faith is lived intentionally, mentorship produces leadership, and every life is challenged to grow, serve, and walk boldly in God’s purpose. We believe in empowering individuals to discover their unique gifts and use them for God’s glory. Join us on this incredible adventure where miracles unfold and destinies are awakened.
