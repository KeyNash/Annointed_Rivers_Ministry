Calling and Early Conviction

Bishop Henry's journey is not built on comfort or convinience: It is rooted in a clear calling. From early in his life, he sensed a pull toward ministry that grew stronger with time. in 1994, this calling became unmistakable when he was convicted to fully serve God and dedicate his life to his work. This conviction was not emotional hype, it was a settled decision to live for purpose, obidience and service.

Passion for knoeledge of God

At the corre of the Bishop's ministry is a deep passion to see people truly know God. He believes faith should be grounded in understanding, not ignorance or routine religion. His teachings consistently emphasize growing in the knowledge of God, living out biblical truth, and developing a personal relationship with Jesus Christ that transforms every aspect of life.

Faith Shaped Through Personal Struggles

Bishop Henry's faith has been refined thyrough real-life struggles, particularly financial hardship. Rather than turning away, he chose to trust God completely during seasons of lack. These experices shaped his character and stregnthened his dependance on God, and become a living testimony. Today, he confidently acknowledges God's faithfulness, having seen restorstion and stability where there was once struggle.

Core Values: Humility, Integrity, and Kindness

This are the values that Bishop Henry refuses to compromise under any circumstance. Humility defines his leadership, integrity guides his decisions, and kindness shapes his interactions with peopke. He consistently encourages others not to give up whether they are facing relationship challenges, financial pressure, or marital strugglees urging them to remain steadfast in their faith and commitment to God's plan.

Mission and Spiritual influence

The Bishop's greatest God-given assignment is simple yet profound: to help people know and understand Jesus. Pastor Anorld Bahati

Pastor Anorld Bahati is a humble, dedicated servant of God whose leadership is marked by character, vision, and an unshakable commitment to building people. His journey is defined by quiet strength, discipline, and a heart that reflects Christ in both action and attitude.

Pastor Anorld describes humility as the foundation of everything he does. He believes proud people don't build, and ministry requires the heart of Christ, a heart willing to serve, listen, and grow. When people meet him, he wants them to feel humanity, authenticity, accessibility, and the ability to connect deeply, beyond titles or positions.

Vision is another key aspect of Pastor Anorld's leadership. He sees beyond the present moment, always looking to the future with hope and purpose. His vision is not just about growth in numbers, but growth in depth - deeper faith, deeper relationships, and a deeper impact on the community. He inspires those around him to dream big, work hard, and stay focused on the greater purpose God has for their lives.

Pastor Anorld is shaped into the man he is today by his background, that is:

  • Parenting

    • Growing up in a disciplined home, Pastor Anorld learned the value of respect, responsibility, and hard work from his parents. Their guidance instilled in him a strong moral compass and a desire to lead by example.

  • Academics

    • Education has played a significant role in shaping Pastor Anorld's leadership style. His academic journey taught him critical thinking, effective communication, and the importance of continuous learning. These skills have been invaluable in his ministry, allowing him to navigate challenges with wisdom and clarity.

  • Experience with God

    • Above all, Pastor Anorld's relationship with God is the cornerstone of his leadership. His experiences in prayer, worship, and service have deepened his faith and strengthened his resolve to follow God's calling. This spiritual foundation empowers him to lead with compassion, integrity, and a steadfast commitment to God's will.

When pastor Anorld was 7 years old, he found out that he was passionate about serving God. From that moment, he dedicated his life to ministry, seeking to make a difference in the lives of others. In 2015, he had a close encounter with God, he says that God spoke to him clearly that He was calling him.

Pastor Anorld id guided by integrity in all aspects of his life. He believes that true leadership is built on trust, and trust is earned through consistent honesty and ethical behavior. He says that even when no one is watching, God is always watching, and that awareness keeps him grounded and focused on doing what is right.

Pastor Anorld has learned the hard way that patience is a vital virtue in leadership and ministry. He understands that growth takes time, whether it's personal growth, spiritual growth, or the growth of a ministry. He also understands that God works in His own timing, and waiting on God's timing is essential for success. God is faithful and so its important to be patient.

What makes his journey unique is the bishop's leadership style, espesially:

Mentorship

He isn't just following:he's being shaped into a leader who will raise other leaders. He describes the bishop's mentorship as:

The Bishop mentors Pastor Anorld not just in ministry matters, but in life matters too.
The bishop supports him through:

Pastorial traaining that teaches:

Through this mentorship, Pastor Anorld is being equipped to not only lead effectively but also to train others in leadership.

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

Originally, our mission begann with one focus:

"Reach the lost"

But the mission has expanded.
Today, we aim to:

  • Reach the lost both locally and globally.
  • Raise leaders who influence society.
  • Restore families, stregnthen marriages, and build communities.
  • Train believers to serve God with skill, character and excellence.

Our mission is to impact lives and transform nations through the power of God's word and the love of Christ.

Our Vision

We are building a ministry that:

  • Establishes strong local churches in Kenya.
  • Expand into multiple nations.
  • Train and release leaders who can plant churches, run ministries, and influence society.
  • Carries the hearts of Christ and the standard of excellence into every enviroment.

Our vision is to see a global movement of believers who are passionate about serving God and making a difference in the world.

Our Values

Our church is built on humility, integrity, and obedience to God, believing that true leadership begins in the heart and is proven when no one is watching. We value mentorship that transforms followers into leaders, excellence in how we serve God and people, and generosity that opens doors and creates platforms for growth. With a global vision beyond borders, we are committed to spiritual and practical transformation, patience in God’s timing, and unity through diverse ministries, equipping believers to influence society and nations for Christ.
