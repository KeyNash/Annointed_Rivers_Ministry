About Us

Get to know our church, our leadership, and our mission.

Our Identity

Who We Are

We are a Christ-centered, excellence-driven, globally focused ministry called to raise leaders, transform lives, and impact nations.

01

A Global Vision

We don't limit ourselves to Kenya. Our eyes are on many nations—Africa, Europe, the Americas and beyond. We believe God has called us to build an international work that spreads His message across the world.

02

Zeal for God

We serve God with fire. We move with urgency, passion, and unwavering commitment to fulfill divine assignments. Our worship is vibrant, our prayers are fervent, and our service is wholehearted.

03

Culture of Excellence

Everything—from administration to ministry, worship to leadership, to how a visitor is welcomed—must reflect God's standard. No shortcuts, no mediocrity. We pursue excellence because we serve an excellent God.

Our Team

Our Leadership

Senior Pastor Henry Jahonga

Founder & Senior Pastor

Calling and Early Conviction

Bishop Henry's journey is not built on comfort or convenience—it is rooted in a clear calling. From early in his life, he sensed a pull toward ministry that grew stronger with time. In 1994, this calling became unmistakable when he was convicted to fully serve God and dedicate his life to His work.

Passion for Knowledge of God

At the core of the Bishop's ministry is a deep passion to see people truly know God. He believes faith should be grounded in understanding, not ignorance or routine religion. His teachings consistently emphasize growing in the knowledge of God.

Faith Shaped Through Struggles

Bishop Henry's faith has been refined through real-life struggles, particularly financial hardship. Rather than turning away, he chose to trust God completely during seasons of lack, becoming a living testimony of God's faithfulness.

Core Values

Humility Integrity Kindness

Pastor Arnold Bahati

Associate Pastor

Character and Vision

Pastor Arnold is a humble, dedicated servant of God whose leadership is marked by character, vision, and an unshakable commitment to building people. His journey is defined by quiet strength, discipline, and a heart that reflects Christ.

Foundational Values

Shaped by godly parenting, academic excellence, and deep experiences with God, Pastor Arnold leads with integrity and patience. He understands that growth takes time and that God works in His own timing.

Mentorship Journey

Under Bishop Henry's mentorship, Pastor Arnold is being equipped through hands-on training, coaching, and real leadership practice—transforming from follower to leader to trainer of other leaders.
