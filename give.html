Give to God

Giving is an act of worship and obedience to God. At Anointed Rivers Ministry, we provide various ways for you to give and support the work of the ministry.

Your contributions help us reach more people with the message of hope and salvation, support our community programs, and maintain our facilities. Whether you choose to give online, in person, or through other means, your generosity makes a difference. Thank you for partnering with us in this mission.
